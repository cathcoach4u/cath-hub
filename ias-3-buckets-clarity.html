Where The Lines Blurred

Understanding why the confusion happened

Bucket 1: IAS Work

"Build AI tools and efficiencies for IAS"

OVERLAP:
Both say "business planning" + "AI tools"

Bucket 3: Coach4U App

"Business planning app built by Coach4U"

🔍

The Overlap

Both involve "business planning" and "AI tools" - the language sounds identical even though they're fundamentally different things.

⚠

The Trigger

Demoing the Coach4U app inside an IAS session blurred the boundary between "what we're building for you" and "what I've built separately."

✔

The Fix

Be explicit going forward: "This is IAS work (Bucket 1)" vs "This is a separate product I offer (Bucket 3)." Clear labels, clear boundaries.
